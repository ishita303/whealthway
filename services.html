   Find a Friend

When you join as a member you will have the option to let us know what is wrong with you, where you live, your interests, your age and what help you require. We can provide a list of members with similar health problems that you can connect to and build relationships with for support. This will allow you to find likeminded friends who fully understand what you are going through, and you can help each other.

Find a Professional

On your road to recovery, you may need professional medical help that you know you can trust. Finding support easily accessible to you is critical and therefore let us help by providing vetted and rated professionals. You do it for a range of other things in your life such as choosing a restaurant, car or hotel, why wouldn't you for medical help?

Find Products

We will develop a range of products that are valuable to the recovery of our members at all stages of your journeys. As we continue our evolution we will recommend products that could be relevant to your recovery based on feedback from other members. Who knows we may even look into developing and designing our own Whealthway range based upon our members demands or needs. Watch this space!!
